Ingredients:
 - 1 lemon
 - 1 avocato
 - 1 onion
 - 5 slices of cured ham

Directions:
 1. Prepare the avocato, scooping the pulp.
 
 2. Mash the onion, the avocato and the slices of cured ham.

 3. Add juice from the lemon.

 4. Taste and add salt or more cured ham, lemon or garlic for making it your own version.

 5. Enjoy!
